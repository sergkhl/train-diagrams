## ESSA (Evolutionary Strategies for Scalable Alignment)
is a 2025 AI research framework designed to fine-tune Large Language Models (LLMs) without relying on traditional gradient-based backpropagation.
Instead of calculating gradients, ESSA uses evolutionary strategies and black-box optimization to align models, making it highly efficient, parallelizable, and suitable for low-memory environments.
https://arxiv.org/pdf/2507.04453
